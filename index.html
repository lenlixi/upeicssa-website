University of Prince Edward Island Chinese Students & Scholars Association

The University of Prince Edward Island Chinese Students and Scholars Association (UPEI CSSA) is a student organization certified by the Education Section of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Canada, and an official association under the UPEI Student Union. As a non-profit, student-run organization, UPEI CSSA is dedicated to serving Chinese students and scholars studying and working in the Prince Edward Island region. The association strives to strengthen the cohesion of the Chinese community on campus and to promote cultural exchange between China and Canada.
